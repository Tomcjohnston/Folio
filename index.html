Are.na

I'm a graphic designer living in Aotearoa, New Zealand. I hold a Bachelor of Design (FCH), majoring in Visual Communication Design from Massey University Toi Rauwhārangi. Previously I studied at ACG Yoobee School of Design receiving an Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design & Digital Media.

Previous experience includes in-house designer at Taupo District Council; collaboration with Bad News Books and Suite Gallery. Currently freelancing in Tāmaki Makaurau., Auckland.
